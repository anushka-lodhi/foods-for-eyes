FOODS FOR BETTER EYE HEALTH AND GOOD VISION

Foods play a crucial role in maintaining eye health, as they provide essential nutrients that protect against vision problems and support overall eye function.

Here are some key nutrients and the foods that contain them:

  • Vitamin A: Carrots, sweet potatoes, spinach, kale, and butternut squash
  • Vitamin C: Oranges, strawberries, bell peppers, and broccoli
  • Vitamin E: Almonds, sunflower seeds, and spinach
  • Zinc: Beef, oysters, and chickpeas
  • Lutein and zeaxanthin: Spinach, kale, and collard greens
  • Omega-3 fatty acids: Salmon, walnuts, and flaxseeds
  • Antioxidants: Blueberries, blackberries, and raspberries
  • Anthocyanins: Black currants, bilberries, and cherries
  • Beta-carotene: Sweet potatoes, carrots, and cantaloupe
  • Protein: Lean meats, poultry, fish, and legumes
  • Fiber: Whole grains, fruits, and vegetables
  • Water: Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and support eye function

By incorporating these foods into your diet, you can help protect your eyes and maintain good vision for years to come.
